Sustainable Development Goals

SDG 1 : No Poverty

SDG 1 aims to eradicate every form of extreme poverty including the lack of food, clean drinking water, and sanitation. In this effort, we are currently providing basic services and job opportunities to impoverished areas in India.

SDG 2 : Zero Huger

Goal 2 aims to End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition and promote sustainable agriculture. Towards this we are reaching out to people with rations at minimal costs.

SDG 3 : Good Health and Well Being

Goal 3 aims to ensure healthy lives and promotes well being. To achieve this we have launched a project "Niramaya" that conducts health checkup camps and provides free treatment to common ailments.

SDG 5 : Gender Equality

SDG 5 aims to achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls. To achieve this we have partnered with "Isha" : an enterprise that makes football boots for women. We strive to empower women and girls to play the sports they love without equipment holding them back.

SDG 6 aims to ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.
